Who We Are

Welcome to Gadgy, your number one source for all things ie: shoes, bags, phones. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of product, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 1990 by Ibrahim ImadUddin, Gadgy has come a long way from its beginnings in a Pakistan. When the founder first started out, his passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for his fellow musicians drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the watches industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
